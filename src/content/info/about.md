GK Bakes was born out of a simple conviction: that a great loaf of bread or a perfectly laminated pastry can make someone's whole day. Founded in 2018 by Geetha Krishnamurthy, the bakery started as a weekend pop-up at the local farmers' market and quickly grew into the beloved neighbourhood fixture it is today.

Everything is made from scratch each morning — no par-baked shortcuts, no artificial preservatives. Geetha trained in classic French pastry before returning home to blend those techniques with the warm spice profiles of her South Indian heritage.

Come in. Pull up a chair. The kettle is always on.
